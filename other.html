Signs of Stigmata

The signs of stigmata are depictions of Christ's wounds on the cross. The five wounds include the piercing of each hand, each foot, and Christ's side.

Tomb of a Vendor of Oil

The tomb of a vendor of oil with various images depicting an embalmed body descending into the tomb, a small house, a candlestick structure fitted with oil-buring funeral lamps, and a scale.

Veil of Veronica

Although not mentioned in the scriptures, the Veil of Veronica refers to a matron of Jerusalem who accompanied Christ on the road to Calvary. The story tells of her offering Christ a towel and on which he left an imprint of his own face, with which she eventually brought to Rome to be venerated as a relic of Christ.
