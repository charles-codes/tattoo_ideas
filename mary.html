Mother Mary

The Virgin Mary, the Mother of God, is one of the most highly revered figures in Christianity. She is also one of the most broadly depicted throughout Christendom. Here we see several depictions which include nimbus', aureoloes, and seven doves representing the seven gifts of the Holy Spirit.
